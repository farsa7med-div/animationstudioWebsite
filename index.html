How We Move.

What types of animation do you specialise in?

We specialise in a variety of animated videos, including 2D animation, 3D cgi animation, motion graphics, motion design, hand drawn, vector, and augmented reality (AR) experiences. This creative approach covers tv adverts, childrens tv series, character animation, brand development, storyboarding, concept development, explainer videos, apps, games, special FX, product animations and social media animations to name just a few. Our skilled animators and production team can bring your ideas to life in the style and format that best suits your project goals.

What does your animation production process look like?

Our animation process starts with a creative meeting, where we discuss your project goals, target audience, and style preferences. We then move on to scriptwriting, storyboarding, and pre-production. Once the concept is established, our animators begin creating the animation, using the relevant industry-standard software and tools. We then add sound effects, music, and voiceover to bring the animation to life. Finally, we work with our clients to ensure that the animated video meets their expectations before delivering the final product.

How much do animations cost to produce?

The cost of animation production can vary depending on the complexity of the project, the length of the animation, and the level of detail required. We offer bespoke pricing for each project to ensure that our clients get the best value for their budget. We work closely with our clients to determine their budget and provide a transparent breakdown of costs, including pre-production, production, post-production, and any additional creative services, such as voiceovers or sound effects. We always strive to provide competitive pricing while maintaining high-quality production standards.

What is Augmented Reality (AR)?

Augmented Reality (AR) is an interactive experience that involves blending virtual elements with the real world. It uses a camera to overlay digital graphics onto a real-life environment, allowing users to interact with them in real-time. AR can be experienced through mobile devices, smart glasses, and other devices. It is often used in marketing and advertising campaigns to create engaging and memorable experiences for consumers. Our animation studio has experience creating animations for AR applications and can work with clients to bring their AR ideas to life.

How do you ensure the animation meets our expectations?

At our animation studio, we make sure to involve our clients in every step of the creative production process to ensure that the final animated video meets their expectations. We begin by having a detailed discussion about your project and what you hope to achieve with your animation. We then provide you with a storyboard, concept art, and animation tests to give you an idea of what the finsihed animated video will look like. We encourage feedback and revisions throughout the process to ensure that we stay on track and create the best possible animation for your needs.

What are the benefits of using animation over filmed footage?

There are several benefits to using animation over regular filmed video footage. First, animation allows for complete creative control over every aspect of the production, from character design to lighting and special effects. Second, animation can convey complex ideas and abstract concepts in a visually engaging way. Third, animation can be used to create a unique and memorable brand identity and finally, animation is highly versatile and can be used in a range of applications, from marketing and advertising to educational and instructional content.

How long does it take to produce an animation?

The time it takes to produce an animated video can vary depending on the complexity of the project, the length of the animation, and the level of detail required. For a basic 2D animation, it can take anywhere from a couple of weeks to a couple of months to complete. More complex animations, such as 3D or augmented reality experiences, can take several months or even up to a year to produce depending on many factors and the overall length of the required content. However, we always work closely with our clients to establish a timeline and keep them informed of progress along the way.

What software and tools do you use to create animations?

Our animation studio uses the latest industry-standard software and tools to create high-quality animations. For motion design we use the Adobe Suite while for 2D animation, we use Adobe Animate, Cel Action, and Adobe After Effects. For 3D cgi animation, we use Autodesk Maya, 3D Studio Max (3DS Max), and Cinema 4D. We also use various plugins and tools to enhance our animations, render animated stills and add special effects. Our animators are proficient in using these tools to create animations that meet our clients' specific needs.

Can you create custom characters & designs for my project?

Yes, we can definitely create custom characters and designs for your project! Our team of experienced animators and designers can work with you to develop unique and memorable characters that fit your brand or vision. We can provide you with various concept designs to choose from and refine them until you are satisfied with the final product. Our team is also skilled in developing original backgrounds and environments that complement the story and characters.

What is the difference between 2D and 3D animation?

The difference between 2D and 3D cgi animation is that 2D animation and motion design is created on a flat plane, while 3D animation is created in a three-dimensional space. 2D animation is a traditional form of animation that involves drawing and animating on a flat surface. It is often used in television cartoons and motion graphics. 3D animation, on the other hand, involves the use of computer-generated images to create realistic-looking animations that can be viewed from any angle. It is commonly used in films, video games, and augmented reality experiences. It is also common to combine 2D and 3D cgi animation techniques to create your video, depending on the requirements of the project.

How do you approach storytelling in your animations?

At our animation studio, we believe that storytelling is at the heart of every great animation. We approach storytelling by first understanding our client's goals and creative objectives. From there, we work to develop a compelling narrative that captures the viewer's attention and drives engagement. We use a range of storytelling techniques, including character development, plot twists, and emotional cues, to create a powerful story that resonates with our audience.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes, we can work within your budget. We understand that every project is unique, and we work with our clients to find a solution that meets their needs and their budget. Our team can help you identify ways to optimise the animation process, reduce costs, and achieve your goals within your budget.
